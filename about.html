Abstract


The Attendance Monitoring System will be used by faculty members (staff) to update attendance according to their assigned courses and Admin to allot and register courses to students. It facilitates to access the attendance information of a particular student in a particular course. This system will also help in evaluating attendance eligibility criteria of a student.
